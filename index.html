Falling Sun

Don't let the sun fall into the abyss!

About the Game

In Falling Sun, you control a glowing sun that is endlessly falling from the sky. Your mission? Keep it alive by safely landing on the platforms below!

Features

  • 📱 Intuitive Tilt Controls: Use your iOS device's motion sensors! Tilt your iPhone or iPad left and right to tilt the platforms and guide the sun safely to the next one.
  • 🎯 Skill & Timing: Land precisely on the platforms so you don't fall into the void. If you miss, the game is over!
  • 🏆 Highscore Hunt: Every successful landing gives you points. Compete with others and see who can achieve the highest score!
